About AES

What we do

AES was founded in 2004 by Conor Sutherland and James Kelly. In the two decades since, members have published in the Andover Economics Review, competed in the Federal Reserve Bank of New York's High School Fed Challenge, and brought policy researchers and practitioners to campus through the colloquium series, where students write and present policy briefs to outside economists and policymakers.

The club runs on three terms. Fall is content-heavy, with weekly meetings on economic theory and policy; winter brings guest speakers and research projects; spring is for papers, the journal, and the research colloquium. New members are welcome at any point in the year.
