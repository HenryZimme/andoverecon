Leadership

The board

AES is run by a student leadership team of eleven members. The board plans curriculum, organizes the speaker series, edits the journal, and recruits new members each fall.

History

Past leadership

AES was founded in Spring 2004 by Conor Sutherland '06 and James Kelly '06. The club has had continuous student leadership since then. The tradition of handing the organization off each December ensures the incoming board has a full term of overlap with the outgoing one before spring leadership transitions take effect.

Past boards are encouraged to stay connected with AES and are welcome to return as colloquium guests after graduation.

Records from boards before 2022 are incomplete. If you served on an earlier board and want to be included, reach out.
